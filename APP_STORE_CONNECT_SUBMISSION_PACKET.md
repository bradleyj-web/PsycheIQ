## Description

PsycheIQ is a psychology and personality assessment experience designed for educational and entertainment use.

Users can explore:
- IQ-style reasoning tests
- archetype and personality pattern assessments
- Enneagram-inspired and pop-culture profile matches

Each result is structured to be practical and readable, with strengths, stress patterns, and relationship insights.

Important notes:
- PsycheIQ is not a medical or clinical diagnostic tool.
- Results are for educational and entertainment purposes.
