PsycheIQ Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

What PsycheIQ Is

PsycheIQ provides educational and entertainment assessments, including IQ-style reasoning, archetype, personality, Enneagram, and fictional character matching tests. PsycheIQ is not a medical, psychological, employment, or clinical evaluation service.

Information You Provide

You can take tests without creating an account. If you choose to save or email a result, the app may ask for your name, email address, age, and sex. In the current version, this account information and saved results are stored on your device using browser storage.

Test Answers and Results

Your answers are used to calculate the result shown in the app. The current app keeps this information on your device unless you choose to email a result to yourself. Emailing a result opens your device email app, where you decide whether to send it.

Purchases

In the Android app, monthly membership purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. Google handles the payment transaction. PsycheIQ receives purchase status information so the app can unlock membership access.

Images and External Content

Some result pages may load public character or historical-person images from sources such as Wikipedia or Wikimedia when a local image is not available. Those services may receive standard technical information such as your IP address and browser or device information when images are requested.

Advertising

PsycheIQ includes member-only sponsor placements that can appear before tests, before results, or before emailing results. If an advertising provider such as Google AdMob is connected later, that provider may process ad identifiers, device information, and ad interaction data according to its own policies.

Data Sharing

PsycheIQ does not sell personal information. Data may be processed by platform services needed to operate the app, such as Google Play Billing for Android purchases, external image hosts for image delivery, email apps when you choose to send a result, and advertising providers if ads are connected.

Children

PsycheIQ is intended for users age 13 and older. The app is not directed to children under 13.
