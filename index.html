FAQ

Useful answers before launch

Are these clinical assessments?

No. The site is designed for education and entertainment. It should not be used as a substitute for a professional evaluation.

Can people retake tests?

Yes. The product is structured around a growing account library, so retakes and new tests can keep the experience fresh.

What happens after the $1 unlock?

Users see the result page with strengths, challenges, situational notes, relationship patterns, parenting tendencies, and example matches.

What should be connected before going live?

A payment provider, user accounts, email receipts, privacy pages, and a backend for saving assessments should be added before real customers use it.
